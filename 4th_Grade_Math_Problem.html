4th Grade Math Problem

Samantha has a garden where she grows flowers and vegetables. She planted 4 rows of flowers, with 8 flowers in each row. She also planted 3 rows of vegetables, with 6 vegetables in each row.

  1. How many flowers did Samantha plant in total?
  2. How many vegetables did she plant in total?
  3. If she decides to add 5 more flowers to one of the rows, how many flowers will she have then?
  4. Samantha uses 2 small bags of soil for each row of flowers, and 3 small bags of soil for each row of vegetables. How many bags of soil does she use in total?
